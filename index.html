About

Business Model: Making one-to-two-page websites for small business owners. Like having an online business card.
Benefits: Affordable, website ownership, free hosting, plus a higher potential for repeat customers and new customers.
Focus: Building a static website for small businesses. Handling the initial setup of the purchased domain and the free hosting platform.
Meaning: A static website doesn't utilize as much data and memory, and a small business doesn't need a book's worth of content; it's straight to the point. we want agency

There's a plethora of options to build websites and it's hard to choose the most feasible option. I've used around 14 different platforms to build or host websites in the last decade. There are platforms with features to move objects around with a mouse but cost $17.00 to $29.00 for monthly subscriptions, or $200 yearly with upgrades or surprise fees. Plus, you don't own the code for that website, so you get stuck paying whatever the cost is or risk losing that website completely. Some platforms require a higher monthly subscription to remove ads or to attach a custom domain. There are plenty of free hosting platforms, but you have to use a subdomain with a bunch of random numbers and letters that nobody will remember. But where is the option for the small business owner that simply wants to showcase some examples of their work at a low price? The business owner or freelancer who gets around 1,000 visitors to their page per month shouldn't pay the same as someone who is getting 10,000 visitors because that's a big difference in bandwidth. We want a sense that we have control over our money and to not be swindled. There's just no need to spend more for less.


I handle the initial setup. I build the website with personalized content for each customer. I provide a block of code that is 100% yours to own. I create an account with a free and vetted hosting platform. I set up a .site or .space or .com domain. Then I hand you the temporary login credentials. All you have to do is update the account's credentials to match your email, password, phone number, and address.
